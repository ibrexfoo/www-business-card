EXPERIENCE

  • Principal Technical Support Engineer and Developer

    Twilio, Inc 2018-2021

    My duties were to lead customers through Twilio’s features which can be complex and not publically visible. To attend daily meetings and provide updates and critical (and constructive) feedback to my co-workers. My job priority was supporting and trailblazing Twilio’s new product at the time Flex.

    My tickets gave an eclectic range of experiences from small solo developers and start-ups to global businesses and personalized accounts. My duties were to listen and empathize with my customer’s concerns and work with my team and guide them in implementing a solution. I would also help educate the customer by shedding a light on our systems so they were more able to self-service in the future.

    Being a senior support engineer I was responsible for answering questions for those onboarding and assigning tickets I thought would be good for those ramping up. For both customers and co-workers, I would explain the processes and logic for products that powered Flex like TaskRouter and (Flex deprecated) Proxy to help prime their Twilio experience.

    My duties also included providing support to a wide range of technical issues that would come up using any of Twilio’s products that had to do with Flex, an omni-communication virtualized contact center. Also responsible for writing or updating tutorials, step by steps and updated and ensured the quality of Flex’s public-facing documentation.

  • Product Documentation Website (Prototype)

    DigitalOcean 2018 - contract

    The coder for the pitch prototype of DigitalOceans product documentation website. The site was made from a Hugo Theme.

  • Head of Engineering for Infrastructure

    Code for America - PDX Brigade 2019

    Responsibilities included managing and leading a team of volunteer engineers to help improve our development process and designing new systems for streamlining our projects and exploring new technology.

  • Software Engineer

    PushP0P 2018

    A lead team member of positive socially impacting engineers responsible for helping teach and manage our SCRUM and lead software designer for the project technical architecture. The project’s focus was a meal-search engine for users with food allergies.

  • Front End Engineer

    Mumba Cloud Aug 2016 - Feb 2017

    An awesome company and good people! They were pushing into the frontiers of the web, they taught me how to SCRUM and what it means to build once and update all.

  • Graphics and Image Editing
